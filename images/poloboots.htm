Product Description

Spanish Boot Company Polo Boots

Single layer polo boots. Wearing these boots you will find them comfortable and easily broken.

They are great in town or country and a fantastic riding boot.

Beautifully made with a leather sole available in dark tan and black also.

  • Knee high leather boot
  • Leather lined
  • Full length front zip
  • Good-year welted leather sole
  • Made in Valverde del Camino, southern Spain
  • Unisex - for men and women

Majority of women tend to take a size down and men sometimes go down . Please check size charts before ordering. Use the length of foot measurement only as a guide very unusual for anyone to go down two full sizes. If unsure please email or call for advice 01344 885373.
